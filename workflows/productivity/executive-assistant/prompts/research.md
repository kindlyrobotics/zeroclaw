# Research Task

Conduct focused research on a given topic.

## Steps

1. Clarify the research question and scope
2. Use browser to search for relevant information
3. Visit 3-5 authoritative sources
4. Extract key findings, data points, and insights
5. Compile into a concise research brief

## Research Brief Format

```
Research: [Topic]
Date: [Date]

Key Findings:
1. [Finding with source]
2. [Finding with source]
3. [Finding with source]

Summary:
[2-3 paragraph synthesis]

Sources:
- [URL 1]
- [URL 2]
- [URL 3]

Confidence: [High/Medium/Low]
Gaps: [What couldn't be verified]
```

6. Deliver via Telegram or email
7. Save to memory (tag "research:[topic]")
