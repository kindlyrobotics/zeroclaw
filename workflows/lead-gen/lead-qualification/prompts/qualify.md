# Qualification Question Flow

Use these as guidelines, not a rigid script. Adapt based on the conversation.

## Opening
- "Thanks for reaching out! What can I help you with today?"
- "Great to hear from you. What's on your mind?"

## Need Discovery
- "Tell me more about [their situation]. What's the biggest challenge?"
- "How are you handling this currently?"
- "What would an ideal solution look like for you?"

## Budget Signals
- "Have you explored any solutions before? What was the investment like?"
- "Do you have a budget range in mind for this?"

## Timeline
- "Is there a deadline driving this?"
- "When would you ideally want to get started?"

## Authority
- "Who else would be involved in this decision?"
- "Would you be the one giving the final go-ahead?"

## Scoring

After gathering info, score internally:
- **Hot** (4/4 BANT): Delegate immediately
- **Warm** (2-3/4 BANT): Continue nurturing, schedule follow-up
- **Cold** (0-1/4 BANT): Provide resources, save to memory for future
