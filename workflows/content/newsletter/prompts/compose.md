# Newsletter Compose and Send Task

Write and send this week's newsletter edition.

## Steps

1. Recall curated research from memory (tag "newsletter:curated")
2. Compose the newsletter following the format in your identity
3. Write a compelling subject line
4. Send the newsletter via email
5. Save the edition summary to memory with tag "newsletter:sent"

## Email Format

- To: your subscriber list (configure in email channel)
- Subject: [Newsletter Name] — [Compelling Subject]
- Body: formatted newsletter content
- Include unsubscribe notice at bottom

## Quality Checks

- Verify all facts against research notes
- Ensure no topic was covered in the last 3 editions
- Keep under 1000 words
- Include at least 3 source links
