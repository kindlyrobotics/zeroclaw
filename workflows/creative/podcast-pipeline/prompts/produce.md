# Weekly Podcast Production Task

Research topics, scout guests, and prepare episode materials.

## Steps

1. Recall recent episodes from memory (tag "podcast:episode")
2. Research trending topics in the niche using browser
3. Select the best topic for next episode:
   - Timely and relevant
   - Not covered in recent episodes
   - Has potential for good guests
4. Draft episode outline:
   - Episode title (compelling, SEO-friendly)
   - 3-5 key talking points
   - Opening hook
   - Potential segments
5. Scout potential guests:
   a. Search for experts on the topic
   b. Research their background and past interviews
   c. Draft personalized outreach email for top 3
6. Prepare interview question bank (10-15 questions)
7. Create promotional assets:
   - Episode teaser for social media
   - Email newsletter blurb
8. Send all materials to host via email
9. Save episode plan to memory (tag "podcast:episode:[number]")
