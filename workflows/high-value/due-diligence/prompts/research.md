# Due Diligence Research Task

Conduct full due diligence on a target company.

## Steps

1. Parse the request for company name and/or URL
2. Research using browser:
   a. Company website — product, pricing, team page, about, blog
   b. LinkedIn — founders, team size, hiring activity
   c. Crunchbase — funding, investors, valuation
   d. Glassdoor — employee reviews, culture signals
   e. News — press coverage, recent developments
   f. Patent databases — IP filings
   g. App stores — if applicable, ratings and downloads
3. Delegate team assessment to `team_researcher`
4. Delegate market analysis to `market_analyst`
5. Compile investment memo following the format in identity
6. Send via email
7. Save to memory (tag "dd:[company]")
